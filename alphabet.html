Alphabet game was developed for a kids 4-9 years old. This game consists 9 Levels to be finish, each level has a letters that the kids need to guess when a kids got a correct answer it will proceed to next level of the game but to make it harder each time the level was completed the next level will let the kids guess more letters until he reach the last level that he needs to guess the whole alphabet by himself.
